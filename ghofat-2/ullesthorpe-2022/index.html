Ullesthorpe 2022

                                         

RETURN TO ULLESTHORPE



GHOFAT was last at Ullesthorpe in 2013, and our return this year proved it to be a popular and successful venue.

Our numbers were reduced from 18 to 16 due to the dreaded COVID striking down one member and one guest.

A rapid over night alteration to the running order on both days resulted in four four balls setting out to challenge the golf course on the first day with a grim determination and light heart in the sure knowledge that things would surely change.

Our four guests this year included part time Spaniards, Bob Fell and David Downes , Kevin Hemmingway, and Keith Simms, son in law to founder member Hugh Evans.

Following the days golf, a lively evening around the large dining table, with rowdy animated conversations was a great finish to an enjoyable day in the company of old friends and new acquaintances.

Day two golf began in fine weather, and luck was with us as it did stay fine all day. Some very reasonable scores were returned, the full results are following.

Generally the consensus of opinion was the the Hotel and staff looked after us very well, the golf course was in good condition and challenging enough for all us old farts.
