Ullesthorpe for 2026?

Update - 1 Jan 2026
 
Hi fellow golfers,
 
it really does seem unbelievable, but it is a fact, we are once again looking for a venue for GHOFAT in 2026. Following 27 years of GHOFAT, and the annual search for a suitable venue that will tick most of the boxes, we are gradually getting to the bottom of the barrel !!
 
It does appear to be an easy task on the face of it. After all we are only looking for a decent golf course are we not? And we all know that there are thousands of great golf courses up and down the country. Well, yes, but we will be seeking a few extra requirements after 27 years of experience, and that sometimes shortens the options somewhat!!
 
So what are we looking for ?
 
1... Some where in the centre of the UK, mainly the Midlands area. Our players come from far and wide and it is impossible to have a short drive for everyone.
,
 
2...We need a Hotel  with accommodation of around 22 rooms.
 
 
3...An interesting, not too demanding golf course, with buggies available.
 
 
4...A decent level of catering for our evening meal in a pleasant restaurant ,and bar services in comfortable surroundings.
 
 
5... Comfortable rooms to a good standard.
 
 
6...All at a price that represents good value.
 
Over the years we have stayed at many of the large group Hotels such as De Vere, Macdonald, Q Group etc, and all have had good and not so good points. Some concerning the golf course, and some relating to the Hotel service, or lack of it!
 
The larger Hotels sometimes offer two golf courses. We have , on many occasions, preferred to play at a venue with only one course, giving us the opportunity to play the same course on the second day, with the knowledge gained the day before fresh in our minds.
 
We have played Ullesthorpe three times now, being a smaller Best Western Hotel it has always provided a friendly and excellent standard of service and accommodation. The golf course is interesting and not too challenging for old farts like us. The cost also is reasonable, when compared with the larger group Hotels. So the first choice for 2026 is Ullesthorpe.
 
Having said that, if any of you have a golf course with an Hotel in mind, that meets the above criteria, I will check it out and get back to you all. To secure the Ullesthorpe Hotel for 2026, I have to pay the holding deposit by the end of January. Members deposit of £35 are not required till mid February, so you all have time to get an advance from your Saturday paper round job to fund your advance payment for the August fun and games.
 
There will be a further bulletin with full details mid February, so stay sharp !!
 
Thats all folks
 
Happy New Year
